I accomplished graduation in Computer Science and Engineering (Knowledge Based System focus) from East West University where I got the opportunity to nurture my passion for machine learning and worked on its statistical approcah in crucial applied science.

Currently, I am working on the manuscript of my MS.c research project which was predicting ASD traits in toddler using DNN, and I am aiming to publish this in a reputed journal. My supervisor is Dr. Md. Nawab Yousuf Ali.

Prior to this, I have an BSc in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Daffodil International University. My undergrad dissertation was based on Acoustic Echo Cancellation for the advancement in Telecommunication under the supervision of Dr. A. K. M. Fazlul Haque.
